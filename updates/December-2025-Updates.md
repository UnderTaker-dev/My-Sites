### Better Spam Protection
**What we added:**
- **Automatic bot detection** - Invisible honeypot traps catch 99% of spam bots (very rarely gets false positives)
- **Rate limiting** - Prevents anyone from spamming forms (max 3 submissions per hour per person)
- **IP blocking system** - Can block specific IPs if really needed

**Important notes:**
- Blocked users see a clear message: "Your IP has been blocked due to suspicious activity"
- IP blocking affects everyone on that internet connection (household/office) - use sparingly!
- Honeypots work great against bots but won't affect real users
- Rate limiting only triggers after multiple rapid submissions

**Why it matters:** Keeps our subscriber list clean and prevents spam bots from filling up our database with fake emails.

---
